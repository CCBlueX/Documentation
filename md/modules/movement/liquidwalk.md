## LiquidWalk

Allows you to walk on water.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── Mode (Mode Selector | default: Vanilla | modes: Vanilla, NoCheatPlus, VerusB3901, Vulcan291)
    └── [Mode: Vulcan291]
        └── Motion (Decimal | default: 0.8 | range: 0.2..1.4)
```

### Settings Details

#### Mode

Select a mode for this feature. Available modes: **Vanilla**, **NoCheatPlus**, **VerusB3901**, **Vulcan291**. Default: **Vanilla**.

##### Mode: Vulcan291

- **Motion** (Decimal) — default: `0.8`; range: `0.2` – `1.4` — Upward motion applied when standing on liquid to keep you on the surface.
