## NoClip

Allows you to move through blocks (let a sand block drop on your head first).

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── Speed (Decimal | default: 0.32 | range: 0.1..0.4)
├── OnlyInVehicle (Toggle | default: false)
└── DisableOnSetback (Toggle | default: true)
```

### Settings Details

- **Speed** (Decimal) — default: `0.32`; range: `0.1` – `0.4` — Controls the movement speed when flying through blocks.
- **OnlyInVehicle** (Toggle) — default: `false` — Only activates NoClip when riding a vehicle.
- **DisableOnSetback** (Toggle) — default: `true` — Automatically disables the module when a server setback packet is detected.
