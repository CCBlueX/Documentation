## AntiLevitation

AntiLevitation cancels the movement changes caused by the Levitation and Slow Falling status effects. Normally, Levitation forces you upward (and is often used as a trap or by certain mob attacks), while Slow Falling reduces your downward speed. With this module on, those effects no longer push you around — you keep your normal vertical movement even while the effect icons are still showing.

This is handy on servers or in situations where opponents apply Levitation to lift you helplessly into the air, or when you simply want to fall at a regular rate despite a Slow Falling effect. The module works automatically while enabled and needs no setup. Keep in mind that this only neutralizes the movement portion of these effects, so use it whenever you want to stay grounded and in full control of your own ascent and descent.

**Category:** Movement
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
