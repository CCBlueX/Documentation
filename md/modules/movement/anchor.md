## Anchor

Pulls you into safe holes for crystal PvP.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── MaxDistance (Decimal | default: 4.0 | range: 0.1..6.0)
├── HorizontalSpeed (Decimal | default: 0.3 | range: 0.0..10.0)
└── VerticalSpeed (Decimal | default: 0.1 | range: 0.0..10.0)
```

### Settings Details

- **MaxDistance** (Decimal) — default: `4.0`; range: `0.1` – `6.0` — Maximum distance to search for a safe hole to pull you into.
- **HorizontalSpeed** (Decimal) — default: `0.3`; range: `0.0` – `10.0` — Horizontal movement speed when being pulled toward a hole.
- **VerticalSpeed** (Decimal) — default: `0.1`; range: `0.0` – `10.0` — Vertical movement speed when being pulled toward a hole.
