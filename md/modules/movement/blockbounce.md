## BlockBounce

Allows you to bounce higher on bouncy blocks.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── Motion (Decimal | default: 0.42 | range: 0.2..2.0)
```

### Settings Details

- **Motion** (Decimal) — default: `0.42`; range: `0.2` – `2.0` — Additional upward motion added to your jump when standing on a bouncy block (slime, bed, or honey).
