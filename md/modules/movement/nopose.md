## NoPose

Prevents entity pose changes for low version servers.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── NoSwim (Toggle | default: false)
└── SneakHeight (Choice | default: ONEFIFTEEN | options: 1.8, 1.9, 1.15)
```

### Settings Details

- **NoSwim** (Toggle) — default: `false` — Cancels the swimming pose to prevent the crawling animation.
- **SneakHeight** (Choice) — default: `ONEFIFTEEN`; options: `1.8`, `1.9`, `1.15` — Selects the sneaking hitbox height to emulate a specific game version.
