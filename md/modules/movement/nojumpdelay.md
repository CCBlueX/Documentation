## NoJumpDelay

NoJumpDelay removes the built-in cooldown that Minecraft imposes between consecutive jumps. In vanilla Minecraft, there is a short delay after each jump before you can jump again, which limits how quickly you can re-trigger a jump while holding the jump key. With this module enabled, that restriction is lifted, allowing you to jump again immediately after landing (or even mid-air in certain situations).

This module is particularly useful when combined with movement modules such as [Speed](/docs/modules/movement/speed), [Parkour](/docs/modules/movement/parkour), or [HighJump](/docs/modules/movement/highjump), where rapid or continuous jumping is a core part of how those modes function. It can help maintain momentum and reduce the stuttering that the vanilla jump cooldown would otherwise introduce.

**Category:** Movement
**Enabled by default:** Yes

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
