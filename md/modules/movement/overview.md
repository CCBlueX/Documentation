## Movement Modules

Movement modules modify how your character moves through the world. They enable flying, faster movement, climbing walls, and bypassing movement restrictions.

There are **37** modules in this category:

- [AirJump](/docs/Modules/Movement/AirJump) — Allows you to jump mid-air.
- [Anchor](/docs/Modules/Movement/Anchor) — Pulls you into safe holes for crystal PvP.
- [AntiBounce](/docs/Modules/Movement/AntiBounce) — Prevents you from bouncing on blocks.
- [AntiLevitation](/docs/Modules/Movement/AntiLevitation) — Prevents levitation and slow falling effects from affecting you.
- [AvoidHazards](/docs/Modules/Movement/AvoidHazards) — Prevents you from walking into blocks that might affect you negatively.
- [BlockBounce](/docs/Modules/Movement/BlockBounce) — Allows you to bounce higher on bouncy blocks.
- [BlockWalk](/docs/Modules/Movement/BlockWalk) — Allows you to walk on non-full block blocks.
- [Clip](/docs/Modules/Movement/Clip) — Allows you to clip through blocks.
- [ElytraFly](/docs/Modules/Movement/ElytraFly) — Makes controlling an elytra easier.
- [ElytraRecast](/docs/Modules/Movement/ElytraRecast) — Recasts elytra while holding jump key.
- [EntityControl](/docs/Modules/Movement/EntityControl) — Allows you to control rideable entities without a saddle.
- [ExtendedFirework](/docs/Modules/Movement/ExtendedFirework) — Extends the velocity of firework rocket boost.
- [Fly](/docs/Modules/Movement/Fly) — Allows you to fly in survival mode.
- [Freeze](/docs/Modules/Movement/Freeze) — Allows you to freeze yourself in your current position without the server knowing.
- [HighJump](/docs/Modules/Movement/HighJump) — Allows you to jump higher.
- [InventoryMove](/docs/Modules/Movement/InventoryMove) — Allows you to walk while an inventory is opened.
- [LiquidWalk](/docs/Modules/Movement/LiquidWalk) — Allows you to walk on water.
- [LongJump](/docs/Modules/Movement/LongJump) — Allows you to jump further.
- [NoClip](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoClip) — Allows you to move through blocks (let a sand block drop on your head first).
- [NoJumpDelay](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoJumpDelay) — Removes the delay between jumps.
- [NoPose](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoPose) — Prevents entity pose changes for low version servers.
- [NoPush](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoPush) — Protects you from being pushed around by other players.
- [NoSlow](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoSlow) — Cancels slowness effects caused by blocks and using items.
- [NoWeb](/docs/Modules/Movement/NoWeb) — Disables slow down caused by webs.
- [Parkour](/docs/Modules/Movement/Parkour) — Automatically jumps at the very edge of a block.
- [ReverseStep](/docs/Modules/Movement/ReverseStep) — Makes you step down blocks faster.
- [SafeWalk](/docs/Modules/Movement/SafeWalk) — Prevents you from falling down block (as if you were sneaking).
- [Sneak](/docs/Modules/Movement/Sneak) — Automatically makes you sneak constantly.
- [Speed](/docs/Modules/Movement/Speed) — Allows you to run faster.
- [Spider](/docs/Modules/Movement/Spider) — Allows you to climb walls like spiderman.
- [Sprint](/docs/Modules/Movement/Sprint) — Makes you sprint automatically.
- [Step](/docs/Modules/Movement/Step) — Allows you to step up blocks faster.
- [Strafe](/docs/Modules/Movement/Strafe) — Allows you to strafe while being in mid-air.
- [TargetStrafe](/docs/Modules/Movement/TargetStrafe) — Automatically strafes around enemies targeted by KillAura.
- [TerrainSpeed](/docs/Modules/Movement/TerrainSpeed) — Allows you to move faster on specific surfaces.
- [VehicleBoost](/docs/Modules/Movement/VehicleBoost) — Boost you when leaving a vehicle.
- [VehicleControl](/docs/Modules/Movement/VehicleControl) — Allows you to fly and speed while riding an entity (like boats or horses).
