## ExtendedFirework

Extends the velocity of firework rocket boost.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── Mode (Mode Selector | default: Normal | modes: Normal, Custom)
    └── [Mode: Custom]
        └── VelocityMultiplier (3D Position)
```

### Settings Details

#### Mode

Select a mode for this feature. Available modes: **Normal**, **Custom**. Default: **Normal**.

##### Mode: Custom

- **VelocityMultiplier** (3D Position) — Custom velocity multiplier applied to firework rocket boost (X, Y, Z components).
