## ElytraRecast

ElytraRecast lets you instantly re-deploy your elytra in mid-air just by holding the jump key. Normally, after your elytra glide is interrupted — for example when you stop flying or get knocked out of flight — you'd have to tap jump again at the right moment to start gliding once more. With this module on, simply keep the jump key held and it will automatically re-trigger flight for you the moment it's possible.

This is handy for chaining glides, recovering quickly after a bump, or keeping a smooth flight going without precise timing. It only acts when you're actually able to fly: you need a usable elytra equipped in your chest slot, and you can't be creative-flying, riding something, climbing, in water, or under Levitation. A nearly-broken elytra won't be used either, to avoid snapping it.

Pairs well with movement tools like [ElytraFly](/docs/modules/movement/elytrafly) and [ExtendedFirework](/docs/modules/movement/extendedfirework) for longer, more controlled flights.

**Category:** Movement
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
