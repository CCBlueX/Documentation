## NoPush

Protects you from being pushed around by other players.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── PushBy (Multi-Select | default: [Entities, Liquids] | options: Entities, Blocks, FishingRod, Liquids, Sinking)
```

### Settings Details

- **PushBy** (Multi-Select) — default: `Entities`, `Liquids`; options: `Entities`, `Blocks`, `FishingRod`, `Liquids`, `Sinking` — Selects which push sources to block.
