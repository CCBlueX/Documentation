## VehicleBoost

Boost you when leaving a vehicle.

**Category:** Movement  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── HorizontalSpeed (Decimal | default: 2.0 | range: 0.1..10.0)
└── VerticalSpeed (Decimal | default: 1.0 | range: 0.1..5.0)
```

### Settings Details

- **HorizontalSpeed** (Decimal) — default: `2.0`; range: `0.1` – `10.0` — Horizontal boost speed applied when dismounting a vehicle.
- **VerticalSpeed** (Decimal) — default: `1.0`; range: `0.1` – `5.0` — Vertical boost speed applied when dismounting a vehicle.
