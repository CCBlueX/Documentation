## SkinDerp

Makes the different layers of your skin turn on and off.

**Category:** Fun  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── Sync (Toggle | default: false)
├── Delay (Integer | default: 0 | range: 0..20 | ticks)
└── Parts (Multi-Select | default: [Hat, Jacket, LeftPants, RightPants, LeftSleeve, RightSleeve, Cape] | options: Hat, Jacket, LeftPants, RightPants, LeftSleeve, RightSleeve, Cape)
```

### Settings Details

- **Sync** (Toggle) — default: `false`
- **Delay** (Integer) — default: `0`; range: `0` – `20`; unit: ticks
- **Parts** (Multi-Select) — default: `Hat`, `Jacket`, `LeftPants`, `RightPants`, `LeftSleeve`, `RightSleeve`, `Cape`; options: `Hat`, `Jacket`, `LeftPants`, `RightPants`, `LeftSleeve`, `RightSleeve`, `Cape`
