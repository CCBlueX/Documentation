## Fun Modules

Fun modules are purely for entertainment. They include visual gags, noteblock music players, and other non-competitive features.

There are **7** modules in this category:

- [DankBobbing](/docs/Modules/Fun/DankBobbing) — Increases the view bobbing effect.
- [Derp](/docs/Modules/Fun/Derp) — Makes it look as if you were derping around to other players
- [HandDerp](/docs/Modules/Fun/HandDerp) — Switches between main and offhand constantly.
- [Notebot](/docs/Modules/Fun/Notebot) — Automatically plays note block songs from NBS (Note Block Song) file.
- [SkinDerp](/docs/Modules/Fun/SkinDerp) — Makes the different layers of your skin turn on and off.
- [Twerk](/docs/Modules/Fun/Twerk) — Automatically sneaks and unsneaks.
- [Vomit](/docs/Modules/Fun/Vomit) — Vomit items out of your inventory.
