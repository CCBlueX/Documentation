## NoHurtCam

NoHurtCam removes the camera shake effect that plays whenever your character takes damage. By default, Minecraft tilts and briefly jerks the camera view each time you are hit — this module suppresses that animation entirely so your view stays stable during combat.

This is particularly useful in fast-paced PvP scenarios, such as when using [KillAura](/docs/modules/combat/killaura) or [CrystalAura](/docs/modules/combat/crystalaura), where frequent incoming damage would otherwise make the screen constantly shake and interfere with your aim. It is also handy for players who find the effect disorienting or motion-sickness-inducing.

The module has no configurable settings — simply enable it and the hurt camera effect is disabled immediately.

**Category:** Render
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
