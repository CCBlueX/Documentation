## SilentHotbar

Disables showing silently selected items in your hand.

**Category:** Render  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── NoCooldownProgress (Toggle | default: true)
```

### Settings Details

- **NoCooldownProgress** (Toggle) — default: `true` — Hides the cooldown progress bar for silently selected items.
