## NoSwing

Disables the hand swing animation.

**Category:** Render  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── HideFor (Multi-Select | default: [Client, Server] | options: Client, Server)
```

### Settings Details

- **HideFor** (Multi-Select) — default: `Client`, `Server`; options: `Client`, `Server` — Selects whether to hide the swing animation client-side, server-side, or both.
