## QuickPerspectiveSwap

Allows you to quickly change the game's perspective.

**Category:** Render  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── RearView (Toggle | default: false)
```

### Settings Details

- **RearView** (Toggle) — default: `false` — Switches to rear-facing third-person view instead of front-facing.
