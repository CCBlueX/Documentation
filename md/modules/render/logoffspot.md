## LogoffSpot

Allows you to see where other players logged off.

**Category:** Render  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
└── SendInChat (Toggle | default: true)
```

### Settings Details

- **SendInChat** (Toggle) — default: `true` — Also announces detected logoff spots in your chat, not just marking them in the world.
