## AutoF5

AutoF5 automatically switches your camera into third-person view (the same view you'd normally get by pressing F5) whenever you open a container screen such as your inventory or a chest. As soon as the screen closes, your camera returns to whatever perspective you had before. This saves you from manually toggling F5 every time you want to see your character while managing items.

It's mainly a convenience and cosmetic feature — handy if you like watching your character model while sorting your inventory, or if you want a clearer look at your skin and held items during trades. You can narrow down exactly which screens trigger the switch using the two filter groups below.

**Category:** Render
**Enabled by default:** No
