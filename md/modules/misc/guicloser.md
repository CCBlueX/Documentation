## GUICloser

Closes GUIs if their title matches specified patterns.

**Category:** Misc  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── Mode (Choice | default: MATCHES | options: Matches, Contains)
├── Filter (Editable List)
└── PrintScreenTitle (Toggle | default: false)
```

### Settings Details

- **Mode** (Choice) — default: `MATCHES`; options: `Matches`, `Contains`
- **Filter** (Editable List)
- **PrintScreenTitle** (Toggle) — default: `false`
