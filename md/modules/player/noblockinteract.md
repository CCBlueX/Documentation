## NoBlockInteract

NoBlockInteract lets you use items — such as food, potions, bows, or anything else that requires right-clicking — while looking directly at an interactable block, without accidentally opening or activating that block. Normally, right-clicking on a chest, crafting table, door, or similar block triggers its interface instead of using the item in your hand. With this module enabled, that conflict is resolved automatically: the interaction goes to your item, not the block.

This is particularly useful in fast-paced combat scenarios where you need to eat, drink a potion, or draw a bow while standing close to or on top of interactable blocks. It pairs well with modules like [SmartEat](/docs/modules/player/smarteat) or [FastUse](/docs/modules/player/fastuse) that trigger item use automatically, since those modules can otherwise be blocked by nearby interactable surfaces. Note that this module has no effect when you are already sneaking, as vanilla Minecraft already bypasses block interactions in that case.

**Category:** Player
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
