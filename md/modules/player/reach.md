## Reach

Allows you to reach further.

**Category:** Player  
**Enabled by default:** No  

### Settings

Below is the complete tree of all configurable settings for this module.

```
├── Entity (Setting Group)
│   ├── RangeIncrease (Decimal | default: 1.0 | range: 0.0..5.0 | blocks)
│   └── ThroughWallsRange (Decimal | default: 0.0 | range: 0.0..8.0 | blocks)
└── BlockRangeIncrease (Decimal | default: 0.5 | range: 0.0..64.0)
```

### Settings Details

#### Entity

A group of related settings.

- **RangeIncrease** (Decimal) — default: `1.0`; range: `0.0` – `5.0`; unit: blocks — Additional blocks added to entity attack range.
- **ThroughWallsRange** (Decimal) — default: `0.0`; range: `0.0` – `8.0`; unit: blocks — Range at which you can attack entities through walls.

- **BlockRangeIncrease** (Decimal) — default: `0.5`; range: `0.0` – `64.0` — Additional blocks added to block interaction range.
