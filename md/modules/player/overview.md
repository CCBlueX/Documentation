## Player Modules

Player modules automate player actions and inventory management. They handle item usage, chest looting, auto-respawning, and other player utilities.

There are **30** modules in this category:

- [AntiAFK](/docs/Modules/Player/AntiAFK) — Prevents you from being kicked for being AFK.
- [AntiExploit](/docs/Modules/Player/AntiExploit) — Prevents the server from exploiting client-side bugs.
- [AntiVoid](/docs/Modules/Player/AntiVoid) — Protects you from falling into the void.
- [AutoBreak](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoBreak) — Automatically breaks the block you are looking at.
- [AutoBuff](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoBuff) — Automatically buffs yourself using various items.
- [AutoFish](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoFish) — Automatically catches fish when using a rod.
- [AutoQueue](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoQueue) — Automatically enters mini game queues on servers.
- [AutoRespawn](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoRespawn) — Automatically respawns you after dying.
- [AutoShop](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoShop) — Automatically buys specific items in a BedWars shop.
- [AutoWalk](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoWalk) — Automatically makes you walk in the direction you are facing.
- [AutoWindCharge](/docs/Modules/Player/AutoWindCharge) — Automatically boosts you using wind charges when holding jump.
- [Blink](/docs/Modules/Player/Blink) — Makes it look as if you were teleporting to other players.
- [ChestCleaner](/docs/Modules/Player/ChestCleaner) — Automatically drops unwanted items from a chest.
- [ChestStealer](/docs/Modules/Player/ChestStealer) — Automatically steals all items from a chest.
- [Eagle](/docs/Modules/Player/Eagle) — Automatically sneaks at the edge of a block
- [ElytraSwap](/docs/Modules/Player/ElytraSwap) — Allows to quickly replace the chestplate with an elytra and vice versa.
- [FastExp](/docs/Modules/Player/FastExp) — Automatically repairs your armor.
- [FastUse](/docs/Modules/Player/FastUse) — Allows you to use items faster.
- [InventoryCleaner](/docs/Modules/Player/InventoryCleaner) — Automatically sorts your inventory and drops unnecessary items.
- [NoBlockInteract](/docs/Modules/Player/NoBlockInteract) — Prevents you from interacting with blocks.
- [NoEntityInteract](/docs/Modules/Player/NoEntityInteract) — Prevents you from interacting with entities.
- [NoFall](/docs/Modules/Player/NoFall) — Protects you from taking fall damage.
- [NoRotateSet](/docs/Modules/Player/NoRotateSet) — Prevents the server from being able to rotate your head.
- [NoSlotSet](/docs/Modules/Player/NoSlotSet) — Disallows forced slot changes by server.
- [Offhand](/docs/Modules/Player/Offhand) — Manages your offhand.
- [PotionSpoof](/docs/Modules/Player/PotionSpoof) — Allows the player to have potion effects without actually having the potion.
- [Reach](/docs/Modules/Player/Reach) — Allows you to reach further.
- [Replenish](/docs/Modules/Player/Replenish) — Automatically refills your hotbar with items from your inventory when the count drops to a certain threshold.
- [ReportHelper](/docs/Modules/Player/ReportHelper) — Automatically starts a report and confirms it for you.
- [SmartEat](/docs/Modules/Player/SmartEat) — Automatically eats food when your hunger is low.
