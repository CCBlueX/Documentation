## PotionSpoof

Allows the player to have potion effects without actually having the potion.

**Category:** Player  
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings Details

Each potion effect below is a toggleable group. **Enabled** activates the spoofed effect, and **Level** sets the amplifier (1–10).

- **Level** (Integer) — default: `1`; range: `1` – `10`
