## PotionSpoof

PotionSpoof lets you simulate potion effects on your character without actually consuming or holding any potions. Each tick, the client applies whichever effects you have enabled directly to your player, making your character behave as though those potions are active — granting movement bonuses, combat buffs, visual effects, and more — without spending any resources.

This is useful when you want a persistent effect that would normally require continuous potion use, such as keeping Night Vision active at all times, maintaining Speed or Jump Boost during traversal, or holding Fire Resistance without ever needing to brew. Enable the module, then toggle on any individual effects you want and set their amplifier level.

Keep in mind that spoofed effects are client-side. Effects that require server acknowledgement (like Strength boosting actual attack damage sent to the server) may not behave as expected on anti-cheat-protected servers. Effects are automatically removed when you disable the module.

**Category:** Player
**Enabled by default:** No
