## AutoBreak

AutoBreak removes the need to hold down your attack key when mining. While it's enabled, simply look at a block and AutoBreak will start breaking it for you, continuing automatically as long as your crosshair is on a solid block. Look away or finish the block and it stops on its own.

This is handy for repetitive mining tasks where you'd otherwise be holding left-click for long stretches. It only acts on real blocks — it ignores air and empty space — and it respects your normal attack cooldown, so it won't fire while you're still in the brief delay after missing a hit.

If you want blocks to break faster once AutoBreak (or you) start mining, pair it with [FastBreak](/docs/modules/world/fastbreak) or [NoSlowBreak](/docs/modules/world/noslowbreak).

**Category:** Player
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
