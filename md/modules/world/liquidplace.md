## LiquidPlace

LiquidPlace allows you to place blocks directly on top of liquid surfaces such as water and lava. In vanilla Minecraft, attempting to place a block on a liquid simply causes your click to pass through without placing anything — this module overrides that behaviour so the liquid face is treated as a valid placement surface.

This is particularly useful when building structures over oceans, rivers, or lava lakes, or when you need to quickly bridge across a body of liquid without draining it first. It pairs well with modules like [Scaffold](/docs/modules/world/scaffold) or [FastPlace](/docs/modules/world/fastplace) for fast construction over liquid terrain.

**Category:** World
**Enabled by default:** No

### Settings

This module has no configurable settings.
