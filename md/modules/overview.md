## Modules Overview

LiquidBounce organizes its features into **modules** (also called "hacks"). Each module can be toggled on or off, configured with custom settings, and bound to a key for quick access.

Use the **ClickGUI** (default: **RIGHT SHIFT**) or the `.toggle <module>` command to enable/disable modules. Right-click a module in the ClickGUI to access its settings.

### Module Categories

- [Combat](/docs/Modules/Combat) (26 modules) — Combat modules enhance your fighting capabilities.
- [Movement](/docs/Modules/Movement) (37 modules) — Movement modules modify how your character moves through the world.
- [Player](/docs/Modules/Player) (30 modules) — Player modules automate player actions and inventory management.
- [Render](/docs/Modules/Render) (58 modules) — Render modules change what you see on screen.
- [World](/docs/Modules/World) (23 modules) — World modules interact with the game world.
- [Exploit](/docs/Modules/Exploit) (25 modules) — Exploit modules abuse server or protocol behavior.
- [Misc](/docs/Modules/Misc) (20 modules) — Miscellaneous modules provide various utilities that do not fit neatly into other categories, including chat enhancements, team detection, and notification systems.
- [Fun](/docs/Modules/Fun) (7 modules) — Fun modules are purely for entertainment.

**Total: 226 modules**
